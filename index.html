How the FLAMES Calculator Works

FLAMES stands for:

  • F - Friendship
  • L - Love
  • A - Affection
  • M - Marriage
  • E - Enemy
  • S - Sister (Sibling)

Example:

Your name: asd

Your partner's name: abcd

  1. Get the flames count:

    Take the two names (asd and abcd). Remove the common characters (two common characters: 'a', 'd').
    Get the count of the characters that are left (remaining letters: 's', 'b', 'c'). Total: 3.

  2. Get the flames result:

    We take the FLAMES letters ('F', 'L', 'A', 'M', 'E', 'S') and start removing letters using the count.
    Start counting from the left up to 3 and remove the letter at position 3 ('A'). The sequence becomes:

    • FLMES
    • Remove 'S' after counting 3 positions from 'M'. The sequence becomes: FLME
    • Next, remove 'E' to get: FLE
    • Then: FE
    • Finally: F

    So the result is 'Friend' as letter 'F' stands for Friendship in the FLAMES game.
